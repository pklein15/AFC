French Conversation Groups

The Alliance Française of Cincinnati sponsors several conversation groups throughout the Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky region. Feel free to join us to practice your French, celebrate French culture, and meet others from all parts of the world.


While no formal registration is required, we encourage you to make reservations through Meetup if you will attend a conversation, courtesy of the organizer.

Covington at Smoke Justis


Every fourth Tuesday
7:30 - 10:30 PM
