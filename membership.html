Membership

Become a member of the Alliance Française de Cincinnati and meet like-minded people who enjoy all things French, from culture and language, to social activities and conversation. Membership dues provide critical support for our programming. Help promote cross-cultural understanding, while taking advantage of our special member benefits. Membership to the AFD is tax-deductible.

Membership benefits include:

  • Membership in the Alliance Française national & international network
  • Discounted rates to AF Cincinnati events
  • Subsidized admission to our Dîner Français Annuel
  • Free subscription to Culthuretèque online library
  • Membership in the Alliance Française worldwide network
